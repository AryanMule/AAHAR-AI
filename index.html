The Big Eight

Eight major allergens — milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soybeans — account for 90% of food allergic reactions in the U.S.

⚡

Allergies vs Intolerances

Food allergies trigger the immune system and can be life-threatening. Intolerances affect digestion and cause discomfort but aren't usually dangerous.

🌱

Outgrowing Allergies

Many children outgrow allergies to milk, eggs, wheat, and soy. But peanut, tree nut, fish, and shellfish allergies tend to be lifelong.
